Record of Intervention

Only one response using Yahoo was observed.

Title of the intervention

Lack of interest in knowledge sharing sites

Discussion of Results

One week after the date the proposals were submitted, the same questions were posted on Reddit for English and Yahoo Chiebukuro for Japanese. On Yahoo Chiebukuro, the question was viewed 26 times but received only one response. The reason for this, we assume, is that once one person has answered the question, others are no longer motivated to write additional answers or provide new ones. In the case of Reddit, we do not know the number of views, so it is not possible to examine the effect of this result in detail, but we expect that people would not be motivated to respond to serious topics on this site. If there is another opportunity, we would like to ask the question on a platform where there is more discussion, or in an environment where people are compelled to respond.
